The Nemesis Legacy

Nemesis Imports was founded in 2025 by Cason Pewitt, a United States Marine Corps veteran with a deep passion for motorsports and Japanese automotive culture. After serving overseas in Japan, stationed in the smaller city of Iwakuni in Yamaguchi prefecture, Cason discovered the incredible world of JDM performance and community.

During his time in Japan, Cason developed meaningful relationships with local car enthusiasts, people deeply connected to FDJ (Formula Drift Japan), and performance shops that were bringing the absolute best out of these iconic vehicles. This immersion into authentic Japanese car culture opened his eyes to the engineering prestige and racing heritage that defines JDM.

Based in Franklin, Tennessee, Nemesis Imports was created to bring the growing Middle Tennessee and surrounding areas a trustworthy source for all things JDM. Our mission is to stand above other import companies by making these newly US-legal cars easily accessible to anyone drawn to these iconic vehicles and the community that surrounds them.
