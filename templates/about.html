The Nemesis Legacy

Nemesis Imports was founded in 2025 after years of building relationships with Japanese enthusiasts, auction houses, and performance shops.

Based in Franklin, Tennessee, our mission is to make newly US-legal JDM vehicles accessible with complete transparency and genuine passion for the culture.

We serve everyone from serious collectors to grassroots enthusiasts—anyone who shares the love for Japan's automotive golden era.
